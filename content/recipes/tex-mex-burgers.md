 - Form into 4 or 5 patties about 1 / 2 inch thick , and 4 inches in diameter
 - Cook on grill for 5-6 minutes on each side , or in a skillet for 4-5 minutes
 - Spread some guacamole , and jalapeno peppers on each bun
