 - Beat sugar into the softened butter
 - ( i leave this mixture on the counter for half an hour so that the sugar dissolves into the butter properly
 - Then pour this into the flour and beat until mixture is white
 - Drop by spoonful onto parchment papered cookie sheet place a christmas cherry on top and bake at 300-325 until pale brown
 - (not sure exactly how long , probably 10- 15 minutes
 - You might want to double the recipe on this one , because you'll end up not being able to put these down
