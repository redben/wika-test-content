 - Place cabbage and carrots in a colander
 - Rinse thoroughly with cold water to crisp
 - Let drain for 5 minutes
 - Meanwhile , whisk cilantro , vinegar , oil and salt in a large bowl
 - Add cabbage and carrots
 - Toss well to coat
