 - Saute onion , garlic , and berries in butter for 5 minutes
 - Add oil
 - Core the cabbage
 - Put in cabbage
 - Cook 10 minutes , covered , on slow simmer
 - Cook 10 minutes , uncovered
 - Cook until soft
 - Turn to prevent burning
