 - Preheat oven to 375
 - Slice apples
 - Melt margarine
 - In a small bowl , combine all ingredients except apples
 - Stir until well blended
 - Place apples in a 9"-inch baking dish , sprinkle oat mixture on top
 - Bake for about 20 minutes , until top browns
