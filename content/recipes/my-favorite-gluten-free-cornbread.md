 - Sift together dry ingredients into a bowl
 - Add egg , milk and butter
 - Beat until smooth , about 1 minute
 - Do not overbeat !
 - Bake in a greased 8-inch square pan for 20-25 minutes at 425 degrees
 - Serve warm with honey and butter
