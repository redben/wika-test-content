 - Mix butter spread
 - Split hot dogs
 - Fill with butter
 - Mix up biscuit dough
 - Roll out to 8 inch square 1 / 4 inch thick
 - Cut into 8 strips
 - Wrap dough in spiral around dogs
 - Place on greased cookie sheet
 - Bake in hot oven 450 12 minutes
