 - Put vinegar in milk add 2 t melted butter
 - Add egg to milk stir up
 - Add wet to dry
 - Heat griddle with melt butter
 - Spoon 1 tablespoon of batter onto hot skillet
 - Repeat
 - Flip when bubbly
 - Serve with jam on top
