 - Wash and peel fresh mushrooms , slice finely and sprinkle with lemon juice
 - Beat the yolk with salt , pepper and the rest of lemon juice , and put the garlic clove through the garlic press into the yolk mixture
 - Gradually add the olive oil
 - Chop the parsley and add into the dressing
 - Pour the dressing over the mushrooms
