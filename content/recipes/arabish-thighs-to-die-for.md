 - 1
 - Fry onions in 2tbsp of oil
 - 2
 - Add chicken , cook white on 1 side
 - 3
 - Flip chicken , dump harissa over it , fry until it sears
 - 4
 - Add water and tomato sauce
 - 5
 - Sprinkle with cinnamon and honey , add raisins
 - 6
 - Cover , reduce heat to simmer
 - 7
 - Cook at least 45 minutes or until chicken is tender
 - Serve over rice
