    - kosher salt
    - chicken broth
extra:

 - Place chicken into a 4-qt slow cooker
 - Combine remaining ingredients and pour over chicken
 - Cook on low 6-7 hours or high 3-4 hours
 - Slice chicken into strips and return to sauce
 - Serve with rice
