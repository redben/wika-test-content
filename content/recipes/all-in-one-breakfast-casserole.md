 - Mix up eggs
 - Add a dash of salt and pepper
 - Cook bacon / sausage
 - Drain
 - Get out a baking dish
 - Put stuffing mix on the bottom of the dish
 - Add the hash browns
 - Add eggs
 - Add cooked bacon / sausage
 - Top with cheese
 - Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 45 to 50 minutes or until set
