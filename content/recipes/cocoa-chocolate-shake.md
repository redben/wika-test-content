 - Place water , cocoa and sugar in blender container
 - Cover
 - Blend briefly on low speed
 - Add milk
 - Cover
 - Blend on high speed until thoroughly blended
 - Add ice cream
 - Cover
 - Blend until smooth
 - Serve immediately
