 - Place 1 inch of water in large saucepan
 - Add brussels sprouts and carrots
 - Bring to a boil
 - Reduce heat
 - Cover and simmer 8-12 minutes or until crisp tender
 - Drain
 - Stir in remaining ingredients and serve
