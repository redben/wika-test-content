 - Cut up onion , garlic and ham
 - Rinse beans and remove any that are discolored
 - Place all ingredients in pressure cooker with seasonings to taste
 - Turn on high pressure for 25 minutes
 - Remove bay leaf
 - Serve with cornbread !
