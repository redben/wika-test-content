 - In a big skillet over medium heat , bring alfredo sauce and broth to a boil
 - Add the noodles
 - Cover and cook 7-8 minutes
 - Decrease heat
 - Stir in peas and pepper
 - Cover and cook 4 minutes longer or until noodles are tender
 - Stir in tuna
 - Heat through
