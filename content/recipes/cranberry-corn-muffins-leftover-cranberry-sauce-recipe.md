 - Preheat oven to 400 degrees
 - Whisk eggs
 - Add in milk and cranberry sauce and mix well
 - Stir in cheese and muffin mix
 - Rest batter for 5 minutes
 - Grease a muffin pan
 - Fill muffin tins 1 / 2 to 3 / 4 full of batter and bake for 15-20 minutes
