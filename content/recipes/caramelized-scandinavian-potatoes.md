 - In a nonstick skillet , over medium-high heat , heat the sugar , water and salt until the sugar has melted and the mixture forms a light brown syrup , 5-7 minutes , stirring constantly
 - Reduce the heat to low , then add the potatoes , coating them with the syrup
 - Heat for 6-8 minutes or until the potatoes are heated through and evenly coated , stirring constantly
