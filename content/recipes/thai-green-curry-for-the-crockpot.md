 - Mix the coconut milk and curry paste with a whisk
 - Add everything in the crock pot except for peas and cilantro
 - Cook low 6 to 8 hours or high 3 to 4 hours
 - Add cilantro and peas at the last 15 minutes
 - Remove lemon grass
