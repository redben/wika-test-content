 - Brown the hamburger in a pan until done then drain and set aside
 - Prepare the simply potatoes , traditional mashed potatoes as directed then place in a 9x12 cassorole glass pan spread evenly in the pan
 - Layer the browned hambuger on top of the mashed potatos evenly
 - Prepare the white pepper gravy as directed add the can of mushroom soup into the gravy , mix well then pour on top of burger and mashed potaotos
 - Bake for 1 / 2 hour
 - Then top with the 2 cups of shredded cheese and bake for another 5 minutes or until melted
 - Serve
