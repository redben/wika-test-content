 - Preheat oven to 475
 - Peel rutabaga and cut into fries as you would with potatoes
 - Place into large bowl
 - Coat them with the oil
 - Add seasonings and combine
 - Place on large baking sheet , lined with non stick foil
 - Should be one layer
 - Bake 30-40 minute
 - Serve hot
