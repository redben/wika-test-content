 - In a 5-quart saucepan , brown sausage in oil
 - Push sausage to one side and add beef
 - Crumble beef with a fork until it loses is red color
 - Stir in onion , garlic , tomatoes , tomato paste , and basil
 - Simmer for 2 hours
 - Add salt and pepper
