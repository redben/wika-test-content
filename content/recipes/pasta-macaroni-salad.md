 - Cook pasta to package
 - Drain and rinse with cold water
 - Set pasta to the side make sure all water is removed
 - In a big bowl combine mayo , buttermilk , dill , garlic powder , salt and pepper
 - Stir to combine
 - Stir in pasta , celery , cucumber , scallions and sweet pepper
 - Cover and chill for 2 - 24hours
 - If a little dry just before serving add small amount of buttermilk
