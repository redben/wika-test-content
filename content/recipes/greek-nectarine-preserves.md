 - Peel and seed nectarines
 - Cut into chunks
 - Boil in water until soft
 - Add sugar
 - Stir and watch to prevent burning
 - Cook 30 minutes
 - Last 5 minutes add brandy , vanilla , and lemon juice
 - Pour in hot sterile jars
 - Seal
