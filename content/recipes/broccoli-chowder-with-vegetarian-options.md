 - In a large saucepan , combine the first six ingredients
 - Bring to a boil
 - Reduce heat
 - Cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes or until vegetables are tender
 - Add 3 cups milk , bouillon , worcestershire sauce , salt and pepper
 - In a small bowl , combine flour and remaining milk until smooth
 - Gradually stir into soup
 - Bring to a boil
 - Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened
 - Remove from the heat
 - Stir in cheese just until melted
 - Enjoy !
 - Yield: 6 servings
