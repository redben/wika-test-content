 - Butter hamburger buns lightly and grill until lightly browned
 - Take out of pan
 - Saute onions and bell pepper in what is left of butter after you butter the hamburger buns
 - Make 6 patties
 - Grill patties until done
 - Add sauteed veggies
 - Top with cheese
 - Cover to let cheese melt
