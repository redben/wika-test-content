Magic Potion Cocktail

14-12-2011

Pomegranates are a rich source of wrinkle-reducing plant chemical ellagic acid. use the seeds to top a dip, sprinkle them on salad or make this festive holiday sangria!

  • In a large pitcher or punch bowl , combine riesling , pomegranate juice and cointreau
  • Add orange slices , apple and pomegranate seeds
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours or overnight
  • Divide among glasses and top off with seltzer or club soda
