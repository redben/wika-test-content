Smoked Tuna Dip

18-01-2010

Want to wow your guests during this year’s round of holiday parties? serve them this smoked tuna dip and watch the compliments flood in. they’ll never know that you whipped it up ten minutes before they arrived!

  • Blend all ingredients together well using a stand mixer or hand mixer
  • Taste , and adjust seasonings to your liking
  • Be careful adding more liquid smoke , a little goes a long way ! when ready to serve , try adding a light sprinkling of paprika over the top for decoration
