Mike S Christmas Nog

24-10-2013

Yummy adult milkshake! this makes a lot for a party....feel free to reduce to adapt to your number of servings! prep time is ice cream softening time!

  • Spoon the softened ice cream into a punch bowl
  • Stir with a spoon until the ice cream is smooth
  • Pour the brand and kahlua over the ice cream
  • Stir gently , just until blended
  • Sprinkle with nutmeg
