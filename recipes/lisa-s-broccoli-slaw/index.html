Lisa S Broccoli Slaw

21-02-2012

Until yesterday, i hated broccoli. then my sister brought this to a get-together and i loved it. never thought i'd willingly eat broccoli, but out i went to get the ingredients to make some last night. thanks lisa!

  • Fry and crumble bacon
  • Drain
  • Mix mayo , sugar & vinegar
  • Pour over slaw
  • Mix in bacon , sunflower seeds and raisins
  • Chill and enjoy !
