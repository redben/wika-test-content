Figs Wrapped In Country Ham

16-07-2011

A very simple appetizer that was served at food tasting event we attended. the simplicity of this recipe allows the quality of the ingredients to shine.

  • Preheat the broiler and lightly grease a broiler pan
  • Set aside
  • Wrap one slice of ham around each fig half and place on the prepared pan with the cut side up
  • Broil 3 minutes
  • Serve drizzled with balsamic vinegar
