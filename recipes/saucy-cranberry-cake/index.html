Saucy Cranberry Cake

29-01-2010

An easy cake that can be served with or without the sauce. with the sauce it makes a sweet, rich dessert. without the sauce, it makes a great breakfast cake, or afternoon snack.

  • For the cake:
  • Mix all ingredients together
  • Spread evenly in a greased 9x13 pan and bake at 350 f for 20-25 minutes
  • For the sauce:
  • Mix all sauce ingredients together
  • Stir over medium heat until it reaches a full boil
  • Boil for three minutes
  • Serve while it's warm !
