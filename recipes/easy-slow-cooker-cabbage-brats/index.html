Easy Slow Cooker Cabbage Brats

02-10-2012

An easy dinner to throw in the slow cooker. we prefer to brown the bratwurst before adding them to the slow cooker. serve with a hearty rye bread.

  • If you prefer , brown sausages on the cook top first
  • Add everything to the slow cooker
  • Stir
  • Cook on high 6 hours
