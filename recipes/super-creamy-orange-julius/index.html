Super Creamy Orange Julius

27-09-2010

My mom introduced this recipe to my sister and i years ago. i have never found another orange julius that tastes better. it's very creamy and frothy!! i love to mix in different fruit (fresh or juice). recipe is from cheapskate gourmet.

  • Place everything but ice cubes into the blender
  • Blend till mixed together
  • Place ice cubes in one at a time
  • Once done drink and enjoy !
