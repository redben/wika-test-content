Creamy Southern Slaw

10-05-2011

From an old cookbook entitled "home cookin" by lela j. clarke. the best coleslaw recipe ever! prep time includes chill time.

  • Grate or chop cabbage finely
  • Mix all ingredients & blend well
  • Chill 3 hours or overnight
  • You may need to add a bit more mayo before serving
