Quick And Easy Vodka Sauce

22-04-2010

I love this served over penne pasta noodles and with freshly grated parmesan cheese. i use my recipe #421238 as the base.make sure the sour cream is at room temperature or it will curdle when you add it to the sauce.

  • In large , heavy skillet , simmer the marinara sauce and vodka , over low heat , stirring often , for 20 minutes
  • Stir in sour cream and continue to simmer , on low heat , until the sauce is heated through
  • Remove from heat and add the parmesan , salt and pepper
