Yummy Cheddar Muffins

19-09-2010

This is a modified version of an alberta cheese recipe. i whipped these up to put in my kids' lunches. they are really tasty and super easy to make. please note that there is 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons of flour. enjoy!

  • Preheat oven to 350 f
  • Grease muffin tins or spray with nonstick cooking spray
  • Combine flour , baking soda and salt in a large bowl and mix well
  • Stir in grated cheese
  • In another big bowl , beat in eggs and whisk in seven last ingredients
  • Pour over dry ingredients
  • Stir until just moistened
  • Spoon into prepared muffin tins
  • Bake for 15 to 20 minutes
  • Serve warm
