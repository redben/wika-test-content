Granola Bars Gluten Free

26-09-2011

Great for the whole family. (this is the gluten free flour mix that i use: 2 cups brown rice flour, 2/3 cup potato starch, 1/3 cup tapioca flour)

  • Preheat oven to 350f
  • Mix sugar and oil
  • Add egg , and then honey , peanut butter , and vanilla
  • In a seperate bowl combine flour , baking soda , salt , ground flax , goji and acai
  • Combine together
  • Mix in rice cereal , fruit / nuts , coconut , chocolate chips
  • Oil a 9 x 13 pan
  • Press mixture into pan
  • Bake for 20 minutes
