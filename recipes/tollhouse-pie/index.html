Tollhouse Pie

04-12-2011

This pie is like a warm tollhouse cookie in a pie shell. it's similar to a pecan pie. i've made this pie nearly every thanks giving for 20 years, but you'll want to cook it more than once a year.

  • Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  • Beat the eggs
  • Mix sugar , brown sugar , and flour with the eggs
  • Mix in the melted butter
  • Fold in the chocolate chips and walnuts
  • Spoon into the unbaked pie shell
  • Bake for about 1 hour , or until a toothpick inserted comes out cleanly
