Strawberry Orange Ade

07-05-2010

I found this recipe online on a scandinavian recipe website. looks like a nice refreshing drink for summer. note -- can use mint instead of basil if you would like

  • Chop strawberries and add to an 8-cup blender pitcher
  • Add orange juice and lemon zest
  • Strip basil leaves and blend with strawberries
  • Garnish with a fresh basil leaf before serving over ice
