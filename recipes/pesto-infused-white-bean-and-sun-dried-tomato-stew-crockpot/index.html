Pesto Infused White Bean And Sun Dried Tomato Stew Crockpot

02-04-2010

The flavours of italy combine in this stew that is especially good served over pasta or with grilled italian bread. if ripe tomatoes are unavailable, use canned tomatoes instead. from fresh from the vegetarian slow cooker.

  • Heat the oil in a medium size skillet over medium heat
  • Add the onion , cover , and cook until softened , about 5 minutes
  • Transfer the onion to a 4 quart slow cooker
  • Add the bell pepper , both kinds of tomatoes , beans , and stock
  • Season with salt and pepper , cover , and cook on low for 6-8 hours
  • Just before serving , stir in the pesto
  • Adjust seasoning
