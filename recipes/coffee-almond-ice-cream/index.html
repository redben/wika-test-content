Coffee Almond Ice Cream

20-04-2011

A recipe from the blog of the family kitchen. this is something i have to try this summer. i love coffee ice cream. the preparation time is the time it has to cool in the fridge.

  • Combine milk , cream , egg yolks , sugar and espresso in a saucepan
  • Whisk well and bring to 170 degrees
  • Then refrigerate well overnight
  • Pour in ice cream maker and churn for 25 minutes
  • Freeze until its firm
