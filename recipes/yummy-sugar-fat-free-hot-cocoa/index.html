Yummy Sugar Fat Free Hot Cocoa

21-07-2010

I looked for a recipe for fat & sugar-free hot cocoa online, but they all had weird ingredients that i didn't have, like pudding. so i made this one up, and it tastes pretty good! it is also very easy and quick. it might be good with a fat-free whipped topping on top.

  • Put milk into saucepan over low / medium heat
  • Once it is fairly warm , add cocoa powder and whisk until fully combined
  • Add splenda and stir
  • Let sit on stove for 5 minutes or until the temperature you want
  • Enjoy !
