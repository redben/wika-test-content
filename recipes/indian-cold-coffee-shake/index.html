Indian Cold Coffee Shake

02-07-2012

Zaar tour 8 is very popular in india right now

  • Add 1 cup ice to blender
  • Add 1 c milk
  • And 1 cup ice cream
  • Blend add 1 / 4 c coffee mixture
  • Blend
  • Pour in glass repeat with 3 other glasses
