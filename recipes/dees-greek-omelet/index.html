Dees Greek Omelet

30-09-2012

Has spinach black olives feta

  • Mix eggs with oregano and pepper
  • In pan saute mushrooms in butter
  • Take out mushrooms
  • Pour eggs in butter
  • Add back mushrooms
  • Add olives spinach and cheese
  • Cook until done
  • Fold over plate
  • Garnish with tomatoes and green onion
