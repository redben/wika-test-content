Bullett Brownies

03-12-2012

This recipe comes from my second mother's family, passed down generation to generation. her maiden name is bullett.

  • Preheat oven to 350f
  • Beat together butter and sugar
  • Add eggs , vanilla and cocoa
  • Sift in flour
  • Stir in walnuts if desired
  • Pour into an 8 x 8 greased brownie pan
  • Bake for 30 minutes
