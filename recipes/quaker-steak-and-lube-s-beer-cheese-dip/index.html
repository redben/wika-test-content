Quaker Steak And Lube S Beer Cheese Dip

12-01-2014

This is a copycat recipe for the dip portion of quaker steak and lubes's lube chips (a crispy batch of natural home style potato chips served with tangy beer-cheese dip spiked with guinness).

  • Combine cream cheese and cheddar cheese in an electric mixer beat on medium until smooth
  • Add garlic and spices
  • Beat well
  • Gradually add beer , beating until blended
  • Chill 1 hour
  • Serve with potato chips , crusty bread or crackers
