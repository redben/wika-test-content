Forde S Diet Soup

25-01-2010

In theory, this burns as many calories to digest as it gives while adding some much needed veggies and fiber to fill you up when you're on a diet. bonus is that it's delicious and comforting.

  • Cut veggies into small pieces and cover with water or v8 juice
  • Boil rapidly for 10 minutes
  • Reduce heat and simmer until veggies are tender
