Berry Whipped Sensation

06-07-2011

This is a light & tasty ice cream type dessert that can be made using any type of berry you enjoy! try raspberries, strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, etc! yum! a cool refreshing treat that is perfect for a hot summer day!

  • Line an 8x4-inch loaf pan with foil
  • Mash 2 cups of berries in a large bowl
  • Stir in sweetened condensed milk , lemon juice and 2 cups whipped topping
  • Pour into loaf pan
  • Top with chopped cookies
  • Press into mixture
  • Cover with foil
  • Freeze at least 6 hours
  • To serve , invert onto plate
  • Remove foil
  • Frost with remaining whipped topping
  • Top with remaining berries & additional oreos
