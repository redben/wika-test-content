Individual Avocado Salads

19-06-2012

Mini salads made in a half of an avocado. i saw this on pinterest, but when i followed the link, it was gone. so, this is my version! these are amazing!

  • Cut avocados in half lengwise , twist to separate and then carefully remove the seed from the other half
  • Combine all other ingredients
  • Spoon into the avocado halves
  • Sprinkle with additional salt and pepper
  • Serve immediately
