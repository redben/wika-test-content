Yum Yum Crockpot Pork Tenderloin

19-09-2011

I found this on another website, but it is the way i have always made my pork tenderloin. so easy and so good. the gravy is amazing over mashed potatoes. you will love the ease of this perfect pork!

  • Put the tenderloin into the crockpot
  • Mix onion soup mix , cream of mushroom soup , and water together and pour the mixture over the tenderloin
  • Set the crockpot on either high or low
  • Serve with rice , noodles or mashed potatoes
  • Enjoy !
