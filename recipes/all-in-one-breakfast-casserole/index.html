All In One Breakfast Casserole

06-02-2012

This is a quick and easy breakfast recipe! unlike other recipes you don't have to let it sit overnight if you don't want to!

  • Mix up eggs
  • Add a dash of salt and pepper
  • Cook bacon / sausage
  • Drain
  • Get out a baking dish
  • Put stuffing mix on the bottom of the dish
  • Add the hash browns
  • Add eggs
  • Add cooked bacon / sausage
  • Top with cheese
  • Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 45 to 50 minutes or until set
