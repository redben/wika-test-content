Pawpaw Papaya Juice

25-05-2011

Typical liberian beverage from .african-recipes-secrets.com for zwt7.

  • Peel the papaya
  • Remove seeds and dice
  • Puree in a food processor or blender
  • Blend together 3 c mashed pawpaw , water , lemon juice , and sugar
  • Mix well , and chill
  • Store in the fridge
