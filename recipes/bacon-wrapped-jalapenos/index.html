Bacon Wrapped Jalapenos

28-06-2010

This is a wonderful,easy, recipe. it's great as an appetizer for a grill-out.

    1. cut jalapenos longways and scoop out the insides
    1. mix cream cheese and cajun seasoning together
    1. spread a good amount of cream cheese mixture in each of the jalapeno halves
    1. wrap each half in a strip of bacon
  • Secure with toothpicks on each end
    1. place on medium heated grill
  • And cook until bacon is done on each side and jalapeno is tender
