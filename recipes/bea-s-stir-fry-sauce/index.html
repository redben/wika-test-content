Bea S Stir Fry Sauce

08-03-2010

Basic sauce for any type stir fry.

  • If using meat in stir fry , cook meat first in 1 / 2 the sauce
  • Set meat aside
  • Cook veggies in remainder of sauce
  • Add cooked meat to mixture and serve
  • 15-minutes-or-less
