Vanishing Oatmeal Raisin Chocolate Chip Cookies

28-08-2012

You must taste them for yourself. they're such a delight!

  • Heat oven to 350 degrees
  • Beat together butter and sugars until creamy
  • Add eggs and vanilla
  • Beat well
  • Add combined flour , baking soda , cinnemon and salt
  • Mix well
  • Stir in oats and raisins , mix well
  • Add chocolate chips and mix together
  • Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets
  • Bake 10-12 minutes or until golden brown
  • Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet
  • Remove to wire rack
