Tunisian Zucchini

18-05-2010

Zwt6 - na*me

  • Combine olive oil and lemon juice , then garlic and caraway seed
  • Salt and pepper
  • Drain zucchini and place into server
  • Pour dressing over zucchini and mix
  • Top with paprika
