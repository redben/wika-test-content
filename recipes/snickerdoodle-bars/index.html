Snickerdoodle Bars

15-03-2010

I have tried some other snickerdoodle bar-type recipes, but they were too sweet and too cake-like for my family's taste. after some experimentation, i came up with these. warning...addictive! they taste even better when they have cooled. these are a great cookie if you want to make something ahead of time that will keep well and taste great for days!

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  • Grease a 9x13 inch pan
  • Mix together first 4 ingredients in a small bowl
  • In a large mixing bowl , beat brown sugar and butter til fluffy
  • Add eggs and beat til well blended then beat in vanilla
  • Stir in flour mixture til well combined
  • Spead mixture in pan
  • Combine granulated sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over the dough
  • Bake for 20-23 minutes
  • Do not overcook
  • The center surface of the bars should stay very slightly depressed when done
