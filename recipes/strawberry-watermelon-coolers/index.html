Strawberry Watermelon Coolers

16-05-2011

Strawberries and watermelon, how can you go wrong? one of the wonderful things about hot weather is this cold drink! adapted from whole foods.

  • Put strawberries , watermelon , ice and sugar into a blender and pure until smooth
  • Pour into tall glasses and top off with sparkling water
  • Garnish with mint and serve
  • Enjoy !
