Healthy Ish Chewy Granola Bars

29-06-2010

I tried for a long time to find a chewy granola bar recipe that my kids enjoyed and that stayed moist. this is what i came up with! it is fairly healthy and you can mess with the additions to get what you want in a granola bar. i will often use sliced almonds instead of cranberries, or chocolate chips instead of raisins. the choices are endless!

  • In a large bowl combine the oats , whole wheat flour , wheat germ , brown sugar , cinnamon , salt , cranberries and raisins
  • Mix thoroughly
  • In a separate bowl combine the unsweetened applesauce , water , flax meal , honey and vanilla
  • Add the wet ingredients to the dry
  • Stir until everything is moistened
  • Place mixture into a greased 9x13 pan
  • Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
  • Cut into bars while still warm , but do not remove from pan until they are cooled completely
  • Store in an airtight container
