Blue Cheese Sauce For Steaks Burgers And Chicken

14-03-2012

A tasty sauce to top beef or chicken.

  • In a small saucepan , saute onion and garlic in butter until tender
  • Sprinkle with flour
  • Stir until blended
  • Gradually add milk
  • Bring to a boil
  • Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened
  • Add blue cheese , wine or chicken broth , white pepper and pepper sauce
  • Heat through
  • Serve sauce over meat
