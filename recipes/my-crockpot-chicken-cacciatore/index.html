My Crockpot Chicken Cacciatore

24-05-2010

We both love this. i usually serve it over penne pasta. it freezes well. could use boneless chicken thighs - probably would not use breast as they dry out in the crockpot. all crockpots are different so, please take this into account.

  • Put all ingriedients into crockpot except chicken
  • Stir until combined
  • Add chicken and stir so the chicken is covered by the sauce mixture
  • Cook on low for 7-8 hours or high for 3 hours
