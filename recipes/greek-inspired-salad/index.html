Greek Inspired Salad

18-03-2010

This is a yummy, plain salad recipe, that features classic ingredients for greek salad. it is quick to make and very refreshing. perfect for a hot summers day. :) i hope youll enjoy it!

  • Combine all the vegetables in a salad bowl
  • Add oil and seasoning
  • Toss to coat
  • Top with feta cheese
  • Serve with a nice crusty bread
